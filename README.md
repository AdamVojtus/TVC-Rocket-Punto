Rocket Engine Punto

Is a project made by Adam Vojtus and Oliver Pawlus, two high school students, studying at SPSIT in Slovakia (current year 2025/2026).
We are colaborating with SOSA (Slovak Organization For Space Activities), TUKE (Technical University of Kosice) - PAIc (Prototype and inovative centre), SPSIT and Segula Technologies.
Key mentoring is provided by Juraj Kolesar.
